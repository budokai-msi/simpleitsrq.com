This hit Hacker News this week: Microsoft Office 2019 and 2021 for Mac are undergoing a view-only conversion. In simple terms, this means that if you're using these versions of Microsoft Office on a Mac, you'll soon be able to view documents, but not edit them. For a small business owner in Sarasota, Bradenton, Venice, Lakewood Ranch, or Nokomis, this is a big deal. ## Short answer: You'll need to upgrade to a newer version of Microsoft Office or find an alternative solution to keep editing your documents.
