AI is getting better at solving hard problems. That does not mean a local business should hand it the whole company. The first useful AI project is usually small, boring, and measurable.

## Start with one workflow

Pick a repeatable process with a clear before-and-after:

- Turning voicemail notes into service tickets
- Summarizing intake forms before a call
- Drafting a first response to common customer questions
- Sorting vendor invoices for human review

Do not start with "make the company more AI-powered." Start with one workflow that wastes time today.

## The three checks

Before a tool touches real customer data, confirm the data rules, identity controls, and review step. Who can paste what into the tool? Which account owns the output? Who checks it before a customer sees it?
