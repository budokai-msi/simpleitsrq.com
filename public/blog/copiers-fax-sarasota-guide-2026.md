## Why Faxing Still Matters in Sarasota and Bradenton

It’s easy to laugh at faxing until you need to send a signed medical authorization to a specialist who won't accept an email attachment "for security reasons." 

In Florida, industries like **healthcare, legal, and insurance** rely on fax because it is still considered a more secure, "point-to-point" transmission method than unencrypted email. For Sarasota businesses, we recommend a hybrid approach: **Fax-over-Email**.

You keep your local Sarasota or Bradenton phone number, but your faxes arrive as PDFs in your inbox. You can still send paper by feeding it into your office copier, but you aren't paying for a dedicated analog phone line that costs $60/month and breaks every time it rains.
