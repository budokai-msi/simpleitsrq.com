## The problem this solves

You have a cyber-insurance renewal somewhere in the next 12 months. The renewal form asks:

> *"Does your organization conduct regular security awareness training for all employees with computer access? Please provide evidence of completion."*

If you answer "no," your premium goes up 20–40%. If you answer "yes" and can't produce evidence on audit, your policy can be rescinded. The carrier is not playing. HIPAA covered practices in Florida have the same requirement from a different direction (HHS OCR audits explicitly check for documented training).

Most Sarasota and Bradenton small offices handle this by buying one of the big-name platforms, assigning it to the office manager, and watching adoption stall at 30% because the platform is designed for enterprise HR departments, not a 14-person dental office where the front-desk coordinator is the de-facto IT person.

The Security Academy is what happens when we run that program for you.

## What's included

### For your staff

- **Monthly 5-minute video training module** — one topic, short enough that nobody skips it. Current library includes phishing, password hygiene, MFA, device security, data handling, USB/removable-media risk, social engineering, physical office security, remote-work safety, vendor-impersonation, wire-fraud, and ransomware response.
- **Quarterly phishing simulation** — a realistic, industry-specific test email. Staff who click get a 60-second coaching moment immediately, not a shaming email.
- **Role-based tracks** — front-desk / clinical / admin / owner each get slightly different scenarios. A practice manager shouldn't be tested on developer-targeted lures.
- **HIPAA-specific modules** for medical, dental, physical therapy, and specialty practices — covered at no extra cost.

### For you (owner / office manager)

- **Admin dashboard** — one page, shows who's done what, who's overdue, who's clicked phishing sims.
- **Monthly executive summary email** — three sentences to your inbox. "Sep: 14/15 staff completed, 1 overdue (Janet, on leave), 0 failed phishing sims, risk score +3 over August."
- **Annual compliance report in Simple IT SRQ branding** — ~8 pages, ready to drop into your Cyber-Insurance Evidence Binder or hand to a HIPAA auditor.
- **Automated enrollment** via your Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace directory. New hires auto-enroll the day they're added. Departures auto-remove.
- **Direct engineer support** from our Sarasota team — ask us anything, we answer in hours not days.

### For your insurance renewal

- Completion data by staff member, with dates
- Curriculum alignment to NIST CSF, HIPAA Security Rule Administrative Safeguards §164.308(a)(5), and Florida FIPA's "reasonable security procedures" standard
- Phishing simulation click-rates trending over time (carriers love seeing downward curves)
- Copy-paste answers for the 12 most common security-training questions on Chubb, Travelers, Beazley, and Hiscox renewal forms

**Q: Is this just a re-seller of [platform X]?**

Above the hood, everything you and your staff see is Simple IT SRQ branded, managed by our Sarasota team, and tied into the rest of your IT stack. We handle setup, tuning, support, and incident triage. You never touch the underlying vendor's portal unless you want to.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: 48 hours from signed agreement to first training module in staff inboxes. We do the M365/Google directory integration, branding setup, and first-month content calibration.

**Q: What if a staff member clicks a real phishing email while enrolled?**
A: We investigate at no extra charge. Clients on Academy get priority on phishing-report intake. If it turned out to be a genuine attempted breach, our incident response kicks in (this is why we integrate with the rest of your IT stack — we can actually see what happened).

**Q: Does this replace my HIPAA training?**
A: Yes. The HIPAA-specific track in Academy satisfies 45 CFR §164.308(a)(5) Security Awareness and Training requirements. You get the documented curriculum + completion evidence a HHS OCR auditor would expect.

**Q: What if my business has fewer than 10 staff?**
A: Waitlist anyway — we're evaluating a sub-10 tier. If there's demand we'll launch one at a slightly higher per-user rate.

**Q: Can I cancel?**
A: Annual billing, no auto-renew. Cancel anytime; paid period runs to completion. Monthly billing, month-to-month.

**Q: Is this going to be another dashboard login my staff forgets?**
A: The training modules come to staff via email — they click a link, watch 5 minutes, done. No separate login unless they want to review past content. Admins have one dashboard. Total tooling addition: zero from the staff's perspective.
