| Jan | $0 | $85 | $0 | $0 | $0 | $85 |
| Feb | $0 | varies | $0 | $0 | $0 | — |
| Mar | ~$480 | $0 | $0 | $0 | $0 | ~$480 |

You customize it with your actual renewal prices and staff count. The total column gives you "will this month be a $200 IT month or a $4,800 one" at a glance — which is information that lets you stop getting surprised.
